Everything You Need to Know

Is Garden Buddy 4U really free? +
Yes! 100% free for home gardeners, forever. No credit card, no limits, no catch. We make money from professional gardeners who use our contractor platform, not from you.
Do I need to be tech-savvy? +
Not at all! If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Garden Buddy 4U. Just add your plants, chat with the AI, and let it remind you when to water. That's it.
Can I use it on my phone? +
Absolutely! Garden Buddy 4U works on any device - phone, tablet, or computer. Your garden data syncs automatically across all your devices.
What if I don't have a professional gardener? +
That's fine! You can use Garden Buddy 4U to manage your own garden completely on your own. The gardener pairing feature is optional - only use it if you work with a professional.
Is my data private? +
Yes. Your garden data belongs to you. It's encrypted, stored securely in the UK, and never sold to anyone. You can export or delete it anytime.
Will there ever be ads or paid features? +
No ads, ever. The home gardener version will always be 100% free with full features. Our business model is charging professional gardeners for their business tools, not charging you for your hobby.
